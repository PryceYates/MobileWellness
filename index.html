What a sedentary day actually looks like

A regular day for the average American worker : roughly nine hours at a desk, four minutes of it not sitting down

  1. 8:45Arrive, sit down
  2. 10:15Bathroom break
    ~2 min
  3. 12:00Lunch, mostly seated
    still sitting
  4. 1:30Snack, at the desk
    still sitting
  5. 3:15Bathroom break
    ~2 min
  6. 5:30Drive home, sit again

This isn't a worst-case day, it's an ordinary one. Two bathroom breaks. A lunch and a snack, both seated. That's roughly what a typical American desk job looks like, and it adds up to about 4 minutes of the day spent not sitting down.
