How the scoring works

Every challenge scores the same way

Whichever challenge you're running, the mechanic underneath is identical — and it's designed so team size never decides the winner.

  1. Everyone logs their own activity under their team name — Marketing, Sales, Engineering, Ops, whatever teams you're running.
  2. Each team's score is the average across everyone on that team who participated — not a raw total. That's what keeps a 6-person team and a 20-person team on equal footing.
  3. Teams compare averages against each other for the length of the challenge. Whoever's chasing the highest team average has something to talk about at the next all-hands.
