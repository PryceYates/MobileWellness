Daily challenge

The Two-Minute Trigger

Pick one recurring cue today, and move for two minutes every single time it happens. Simple enough to start in the next five minutes.

How it works

  1. Agree on one shared cue for the day — every call that ends, every hour on the hour, every time someone posts in the team channel.
  2. Whenever the cue happens, stand up and move for at least two minutes. Stretch, walk the hallway, whatever's available.
  3. Log each one under your team — Marketing, Sales, Engineering, whichever team you're on.
  4. Your team's score for the day is the average number of triggers hit per person — so team size doesn't decide the outcome. Compare your team's average against the other teams running the challenge today.

Why a daily challenge

Daily challenges are the lowest-commitment entry point — you can start and finish one in the same day, which makes them a good way to test a framework before committing to something longer.
